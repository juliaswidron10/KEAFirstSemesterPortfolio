  • DATA COLLECTION, CONTENT PRODUCTION

    Research assignment focused on collecting data and writing summaries, during this project I had to make couple types of research, Including: desk research to learn about educational way to use legos; observation to learn how to find informations by observing real people, I went to the closest train station to take photos and collect data about people that took train that day; interview to learn about interest and everyday life of specific person; survey to learn about life of an international student in Denmark. Content production part of this module included creating content for a blog/page. I decided to make a blog page for a passionate cook/traveler. My content on the website was based on international recipes and traveling.
