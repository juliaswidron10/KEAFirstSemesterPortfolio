  • DATA COLLECTION, CONTENT PRODUCTION

    Group challenge to create an app that solves one of KEA’s student problems. Me and my team decided to work on problem of student facing a problem that could be easily solved with help of other people. Our idea was to create an app that could work as some sort of social media where people could post their problems as well as ads on what they could help other students with. When we had our idea ready we brainstormed ideas for our design, we used crazy 8’s method to sketch out our ideas, then we voted for what we thought are the best ideas and polished them. We decided with our design to stick to existing color scheme of KEA. Our app would have two color modes, dark one and the white one to separate offers of help and ads asking for help in the most user friendly way. We tested our project to make sure that users won’t get confused in the process. One of the challenges of this assignment was also learning how to work remotely on a group project, what’s the best way to divide assignments between all of us as well as what are the best ways to communicate with other members. We worked on our project came out with problems that we could face and possible solutions to them. To summarize our project we had to prepare and present our pitch in front of the class.
