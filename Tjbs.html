  • TJUBANG SKO

    In this group assignment we teamed up to create a promotional video and create a website/visual identity for a local shoe store. Our assignment was mostly focused on creating a video for the client. We were introduced to story boards and filming techniques. My team and I wanted to create an interactive video in which you interact with choices that the main character can make. We wanted to focus on feelings and store mission and what they stand for. During the filming process we also took promotional photos of the shoes that we used as a part of our website and visual identity. I worked mostly on visual aspects of the project, created web and mobile prototype, designed logo, made samples of social media posts and coded the website. During the process of working on the assignment we divided the tasks and then during meetings we showed our work to give each other feed back and approve all the parts of the project together. Then when we finished everything for the client we worked on presentation together and wrote a pitch that we could present to the client later.

  • CODE AND DESIGN

    Tools used in project include: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Adobe suite (photoshop, illustrator, xd, premier pro), trello

  • SKILLS LEARNED DURING THIS ASSIGNMENT

    recording techniques, S.C.R.A.P, recording sounds, video narration, Video terminology (F.E.M.O, b-roll, rule of thirds, 180 degree rule, color temperature, white balance, blacklight and white balance, shot, close up, medium shot, long shot, composition, angle, dolly shot, color correction, smooth cut, hard cut, cross cut, dramaturgy) copyright and licenses, social media content, UX analysis
