  • PHOTOGRAPHY TUTORIAL

    Project where we’ve been given article about photography but also we’ve got assigned to different styles that we had to portrait in our design. I got assigned to the style of arts and crafts. I studied this style of art and decided to base my design on the art of Alfons Mucha. It was very challenging for me to design a logo that would be simple as logo and portrait very colorful and detailed style of arts and crafts. I designed my logo a little bit more detailed to portrait the style more even if it made my logo loose some readability when it’s very small. My design process started with a mood board that I used as inspiration for the logo and then I used these elements to create my website layout and then I made icons that would fit the design. For images that I used, most of them come from free to use websites that i edited and color graded. Some images like the exposure triangle and Kevin Light conditions I created in photoshop. Part of this assignment also included creating style-tile for the website and user testing. During user testing I discovered some mistakes and after feedback I was able to fix those mistakes like adapting font sizes and changing navigation.

  • CODE AND DESIGN

    Tools used in project include: HTML, CSS, Adobe Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, xD)

  • SKILLS LEARNED DURING THIS ASSIGNMENT

    C.R.A.P, basic rules of design (past experience, contrast, proximity, similarity, common fate), using grids with layout, making moodboards and style tile, sketching and designing logo, creating logo in illustrator with pen tool, designing icons, golden ratio, gestalt laws, visual research, desk research, readability, trunk test, think aloud test,
